# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Job seekers, developers, and technical professionals who are actively applying to jobs and want to automate job matching and company research.

## Product Purpose
JobPilot is an AI-powered job hunting assistant that automatically discovers relevant jobs, scores them against the user's profile, and researches companies.

## Brand Personality
Bold, Intelligent, and Seamless. Focus on a high-end, professional tech assistant feel.

## Anti-references
SaaS clichés, hero-metric templates, identical card grids, and gradient text.

## Design Principles
- **Expert Confidence**: Present match scores and dossier insights clearly, without fluff.
- **Intelligent Focus**: Guide the user's eye to high-scoring matched jobs and actionable tasks.
- **Seamless Flow**: Minimize friction between onboarding, profiling, searching, and applying.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA contrast standards, visible focus states, and support for reduced motion.
